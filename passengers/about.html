Our Story

Southrift Services Limited is one of Kenya's leading public transport cooperatives. With years of experience, we connect major towns across the Nairobi, Rift Valley, and western regions with reliable and safe transport solutions. As a fully registered SACCO under NTSA regulations, we bring together PSV owners, drivers, and staff to operate under one disciplined and efficient system.

Southrift Services Limited was founded in the year 2007 by our former Director, the late Christopher Maritim.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable and affordable public transport while promoting cooperative economic growth for our members.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative transport SACCO in East Africa.
